Fried Zucchini

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 2 eggs
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese.
  • 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
  • canola oil for frying

Directions

  1. Trim ends off zucchini and slice into ½" slices.
  2. Beat eggs with salt and pepper to taste. Combine parmesan cheese, bread crumbs and salt & pepper to taste in a separate dish.
  3. Pour oil into a pan to ½" deep and heat over medium heat.
  4. Dredge zucchini in flour, dip in eggs and finally dip in bread crumbs. Repeat with remaining slices.
  5. Add zucchini to the pan, a few at a time (don't crowd) cooking 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels. Season with salt if desired.
